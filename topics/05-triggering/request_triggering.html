Trigger Events

hx-trigger controls when the request fires — on click, on input, on hover, after a delay, and more.

1. Click (default)

hx-trigger="click" — fires when the button is clicked

2. Keyup with delay

hx-trigger="keyup changed delay:500ms" — fires 500 ms after you stop typing

3. Mouse-enter

hx-trigger="mouseenter" — fires when you hover over the element
